About Insurance

The Insurance Corporation is the largest publicly held personal lines property and casualty insurer in America, serving more than 16 million households nationwide.

Founded in 1931, Insurance has been dedicated to protecting our customers from life's uncertainties and preparing them for the future for more than 85 years.



About Mission

Insurance best-in-class products and services help provide innovative protection and retirement solutions to more than 16 million households nationwide.
